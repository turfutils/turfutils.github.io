Turf utils

Alternative scoring lists

Unofficial scoring systems for Turfgame.com.

  1. EQualized scoring
  2. Twenty4 hour rush
  3. Turf a Million
  4. Turf original with extra stats

Examples for how the score is counted for the different lists can be found here

1 - EQualized scoring

The first hiscore list evens out the score by giving the same points for the same work. The amount of zones you take during 24 hours acts as a base for the amounts of points you get for every zone the next 24 hours. This makes it good to turf every day!

2 - Twenty4 hour rush

List two is similar to the first but to get the max score it requires a lot more work. See the example link below.

3 - Turf a Million!

List three has a max roof of one million points. You get bonus for how many zones you have taken this round and how many of them were unique. You also get a bonus for number of kilometers traveled. Sorry, plane rides do not count. 😁

None of the above lists give any points for holding zones. Revisit and assist counts as normal takeover and gives full points.

4 - Original hiscore

The fourth list is just the original score but with some added statistics like how many points per kilometers, how many hours spent turfing and so on. The score is not exactly synced with official hiscore, the pph can get behind a little on users that do not actively turf.
